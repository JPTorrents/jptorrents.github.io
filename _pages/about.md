# About

## Academic profile

Joël Perez-Torrents is a postdoctoral researcher in organizational theory and information systems at McGill University (Desautels Faculty of Management), based in Montréal. He is also affiliated with Centre de Recherche en Gestion, i3-CNRS, Institut Polytechnique Paris.

## Research orientation

His research focuses on epistemic transformations linked to AI in high-expertise environments, especially healthcare. The work combines pragmatist, STS, and organizational theory perspectives with qualitative methods.
