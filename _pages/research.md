# Research

## Research agenda

My research explores how AI tools are integrated into real-world professional workflows, with special attention to healthcare institutions and collaborative decision-making.

## Current projects

### Clinical coordination and AI support

Study of how clinicians adapt AI-supported workflows while preserving accountability and patient-centered care.

### Ethnographic methods for AI evaluation

Development of field-based methods to evaluate AI use beyond benchmark performance.

### Human-AI collaboration frameworks

Comparison of collaboration patterns across teams, roles, and organizational contexts.
